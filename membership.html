Beginner Classes

Beginner classes offer a slower pace focused on building stamina, strength, and flexibility. We offer a free introductory class for newcomers to our studio!

Intermediate Classes

Intermediate classes focus on advanced poses and breathwork, we aim to challenge your physical strength and stamina in our thorough weekly class.

Advanced Classes

The advanced classes take the physical challenging components from yoga, and applying rhythmic movements that are connected to the breathwork and control.

Rules and Regulations

Yoga is gentle and life fulfilling practice meant to be shared and center a community, we work to instill safety and respect in our studios, and each member is expected to follow our studio guidelines. We have a zero-tolerance policy towards physical violence, resulting in immediate termination of membership, see details regarding membership policy here. Numerous and repeated complaints filed against a member will lead to a hold in membership until our staff conducts a membership review. Comments or complaints regarding safety and wellbeing can be directed to our front desk staff or contact us today.

Zen Yoga
Zen Yoga is a private Yoga Studio located in the city of Vancouver.
Our mission is to share the benefits of yoga with our local community.
Our establishment is built on the principle that Yoga is beneficial for anyone and everyone.
